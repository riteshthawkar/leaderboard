How it works

Evaluation pipeline

One GPT-4o judge. Paper-faithful scoring. Three benchmarks unified into comparable rankings.

[01]

Generate responses

Run your model on released questions. For Spatial, generate CoT and no-image conditions.

[02]

Submit predictions

Upload a JSON or CSV file per task. Evaluation is fully offline.

[03]

Judge & extract

GPT-4o extracts answers (perception & imagery) or judges correctness (spatial).

[04]

Score & diagnose

Accuracy, macro averages, random baselines, and spatial robustness diagnostics.

[05]

Rank

Per-benchmark tables and a combined Visual-Cognition Index (VCI).

[A]

Do-You-See-Me

GPT-4o answer extractor → exact / numeric match. Score = macro average of 2D and 3D accuracy.

[B]

Mind's-Eye

GPT-4o answer extractor → MCQ-label match, reported against a random-choice baseline.

[C]

Spatial

GPT-4o as judge on restricted MCQ options. pass@1, greedy decoding, averaged over seeds.
